Our Workout Plans

Choose the workout plan that fits your fitness goals and lifestyle!

Beginner Plan

This plan is designed for those new to fitness or just starting their journey. It focuses on fundamental exercises to build strength, flexibility, and endurance.

  • 3 days per week
  • Full-body workouts
  • Duration: 30-45 minutes per session

Intermediate Plan

The intermediate plan is for those with a foundation in fitness who are looking to challenge themselves. It includes a mix of cardio, strength training, and functional workouts.

  • 4-5 days per week
  • Split workouts (upper body, lower body, core)
  • Increased intensity and volume
  • Duration: 45-60 minutes per session
